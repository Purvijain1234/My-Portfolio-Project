Welcome to Badoli

Your guide to reaching this charming village in Meerut, India.

How to Reach Badoli :

Badoli is easily accessible by road and rail from Meerut and Delhi.

  • By Car: Take NH334 from Meerut and follow the route towards Sardhana. From Sardhana, head towards Badoli.
  • By Train: The nearest railway station is Meerut City Junction. From there, hire a taxi or take a bus to Badoli.
  • By Bus: Regular buses connect Meerut to nearby villages, including Badoli.
